Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 24, 2025

Your privacy is important to us. It is Photo Swipe Clean's policy to respect your privacy regarding any information we may collect from you across our application.

1. Information We Collect

Photo Swipe Clean does NOT collect or store your personal photos on any external server. All photo processing, cleaning, and organization happens locally on your device.

Local Data

We access your photo library solely to provide the functionality of the app (organizing and deleting photos). This data never leaves your device.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data (such as button taps, screen views) to help us improve the app. This data is not linked to your personal identity.

2. Permissions

The app requires the following permissions to function:

  • Photo Library Usage: To display your photos for cleaning and organization.
  • Tracking (Optional): To deliver personalized ads (if applicable) or analytics.

3. Security

Since we do not store your photos on our servers, your personal memories are as secure as your device itself.

4. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
